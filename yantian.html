Location

Shenzhen, China

Scope

Yantian Port Galleria

A Gateway to Maritime Heritage

The Yantian Port Gallery is more than just an exhibition space; it is a narrative journey. Designed as the new cultural landmark for the Yantian Port Group in China, this project aims to bridge the gap between the massive, fast-paced industrial operations of the port and the public's understanding of them.

"Create an immersive educational experience that transforms visitors from passive observers into engaged participants. The gallery tells two interconnected stories: History and the Operation"
